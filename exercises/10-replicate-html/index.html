3 reasons you know you are learning

We area going to explain in this paragraph the 3 most common signs that you should look into yourself to recognize if you are learning:

  1. You are able to complete the exercises by yourself
  2. You understand what the teacher is talking about
  3. You are able to have conversations about the topic
